About company
Ð¡ompany MAXILOGISTIC provides services for the transportation of goods on the territory in Europe, all cities of Russia and the CIS. We offer optimal solutions for the transportation of goods and deliver them to their destination.

Our specialists have been successfully working in the cargo transportation market for over 10 years. The company provides a full range of customs clearance services.

In addition to standard and neutral cargo, we transport goods requiring a certain temperature regime, as well as groupage, dangerous and oversized cargo. The company provides multimodal transportation by water, air and rail transport.

To work with our partners, we use our own vehicles with a carrying capacity of up to 24 tons, and also cooperate with reliable carriers in Russia and Europe. The company provides storage facilities for the consolidation of your cargo. We work for cash and non-cash payments both in rubles and in foreign currency.
